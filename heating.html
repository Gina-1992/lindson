LINDSON HEATING

Heating – a necessity that all of us can’t live without, yet many households experience a breakdown in boilers every year. Like plumbing, it all ties in together with all appliances that run in your house. Whether it’s the cozy radiator, the steaming shower, or the warm water in your sinks, these are all powered by your boiler, so if you feel you’re having an issue with yours, Lindson can help you immediately.

Our Heating Services

Here at Lindson we make sure we are available at any time to assist with any breakdowns you may experience in your households, most frequently and importantly your boiler. We have hands on skilled experts to make sure your heating is back up and running in no time, no matter the problem.

What We Offer

We can assist you in most inspections and fixes to your household heating appliances. These include:

Boiler installations, repairs and servicing

Fault finding

Vent/unvented cylinders

Central heating pumps repair

Central heating controls and installation

Radiator bleeding, leaks, noise fixing and installation

Gas repairs, resting and certificate

Appliance installation, servicing and repairs

Thermostats valve, immersion heater & power flushes
