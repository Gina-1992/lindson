COMPANY HISTORY

For the past 28 years, Stephen Henderson has worked with the building trade energetically; starting off working for a plentiful amount of highly established companies, to breaking out and creating his own business, building up into a highly skillful, trustworthy tradesman with a polished reputation known all around Bristol.

Lindson’s are a family run business, specialising in complete building service, inclusive of all areas of plumbing, heating, construction, repairs, maintenance, and inspections. Our number one objective that we are proud of is ensuring customers’ needs are the priority and fulfilled correctly; whether you’re a new or returning customer, our friendly staff at LBS always make sure any service required (big or small) is carried out beyond it’s highest standard, never leaving any work undone.
